# Dining GA4 Internal Link Click Discovery Notes

Date range reviewed: 2026-04-01 to 2026-06-29

## Question

Can GA4 currently support internal Dining Learn More / venue-card click reporting from the Dining landing page to Dining detail pages?

## Finding

Internal Dining link clicks were not visible in the reviewed GA4 click evidence.

The reviewed outbound click exports showed populated Link URL values for outbound destinations such as SevenRooms, OpenTable, and Grubhub, but did not provide evidence of internal Dining detail-page click URLs such as `/dining/stubborn-seed/`, `/dining/kusa-nori/`, or other internal venue-detail paths.

## Interpretation

GA4 can still report Dining detail-page traffic, but this evidence does not confirm clean click-through reporting from the Dining landing page to internal Dining detail pages.

## Reporting boundary

Do not claim Learn More clicks, internal card clicks, or venue-card click-through rates are reportable unless additional GA4 or GTM evidence confirms those interactions.

## Status

Internal Dining Learn More / card click reporting: Not confirmed from current GA4 evidence.
