# Leadership Summary

## Measurement Caveat

Summer Offers Book Now tracking has been improved and validated. We can now report which offers drove Book Now clicks, which categories users engaged with, and which rate codes were handed off to the booking engine.

The remaining limitation is deeper booking attribution. Booking-engine checkout activity exists in GA4, but Summer Offers rate-code context does not currently appear to persist into checkout or confirmed hotel purchase events. Revenue should therefore be treated as directional until booking-engine attribution is further validated.
